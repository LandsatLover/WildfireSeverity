Summary

This layer represents an approximation of the burn severity of the wildfire that occurred in Tenerife, Canary Islands, on August 15, 2023. Using NBR ratio and the K-means algorithm to extract the burned-unburned mask and classifying the result by quantiles from Low severity to High severity.
